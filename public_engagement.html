Public Engagement

As a scholar at the intersection of political science and public policy, I write reports and op-ed to inform policymakers and the general public. In addition, I frequently communicate my research with the media.

7. "Challenges to Democracy at Home and Abroad." (Baobao Zhang), 2019, Kerry Initiative, Jackson Institute for Global Affairs, Yale University.
